# Part Documentation

This plug fits standard 20mm dogholes and on the underside houses a M3.5 hex nut(Any nut with a 5.8-6mm outer width will do). Depending on filament used and how the part has had time to cool it "should" be a tight press to fit.

If the fitment is too tight, We have had success using a large insert tip on a soldering iron to press them in.

If the fitment is too loose, it still should be snug enough where a drop of super glue into the center of the nut will hold it. We have testing this as well.

## Parts Needed
M3.5 Hex Nuts - [Amazon(US)](https://a.co/d/00CYgKOE)

M3.5 Hex Nuts - [McMaster-Carr](https://www.mcmaster.com/91828A220/)

CA Glue or Hot Glue

Threaded insert tip and soldering iron
